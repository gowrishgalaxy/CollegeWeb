ABOUT COLLEGE

MRIT provides top notch infrastructure with campus-wide WiFi, amply ventilated
and well lit roomy classrooms, well equipped laboratories, clean and hygienic canteen facility.
The MRIT library with its diverse collection of well regarded books,
journals and magazines is a place for students to enrich their knowledge.
Bus facility is available from Mysuru, Mandya, Pandavapura, Bannur, Arakere, Shrirangapattana, Hunsur and Maddur.
With a dedicated faculty and a focus on hands on learning, MRIT aims to be a leading
institute for technological education, providing students with the skills and knowledge
they need to succeed in the competitive world of engineering.

PRINCIPAL

Dr. DJ Ravi
Dr. D J Ravi is the current Principal of Mysuru Royal Institute of Technology (MRIT) in Mandya. He holds a Ph.D. in Speech Processing and has over 32 years of experience in teaching and research. He has published numerous research papers and has been actively involved in academic administration, serving on various boards and committees.

VICE PRINCIPAL

Dr. Abhinandhan K S
Dr. Abhinandhan K S is the current Vice Principal of Mysuru Royal Institute of Technology (MRIT) in Mandya. He holds a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering and has over 12 years of experience in teaching and research. He has taught various subjects including Engineering Graphics, Mechanics of Materials, Design of Machine Elements, Finite Element Methods, Mechanical Measurements and Control Engineering, and Introduction to Python Programming. He is a life member of the Tribology Society of India and the Institute of Engineers.
